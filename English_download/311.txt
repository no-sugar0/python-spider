					<div class="Investment_p">
						Jinan is a coastal open city approved by the State Council, center of the south wing of Bohai Rim, and one of the fifteen sub-provincial cities. It is also the political, economic, cultural, and financial centers of Shandong Province, the third strongest economy-promising province in China. Jinan is an important transportation hub and a logistics center along Beijing-Shanghai economic development axis. Looking ahead, Jinan is committed to the key development goals of "building Four Centers and a modern City of Springs" during the 13th Five-Year Plan.

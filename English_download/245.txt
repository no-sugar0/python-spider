          <p>Entries of the 2019 China Tourism Commodities Contest went on display at Yiwu International Expo Center in Zhejiang province. These well-designed commodities combining the characteristics of the destination and modern aesthetics are popular souvenirs among tourists.</p>
<p>It's one of the various events of the 14th China Yiwu Cultural Products Trade Fair and the 11th China International Tourism Commodities Fair, which were jointly held between April 27 and 30 in the venue.</p>
<p>Within an area of 100,000 square meters, there are 1,277 exhibitors from home and abroad showcasing various commodities in 4,192 exhibition stalls. There are 10 exhibition areas such as intangible culture heritage, animation, comics and games, and creative cultural souvenirs.</p>

YANGON  Two Reuters journalists who were arrested for illegally obtaining and possessing government documents in December 2017 were released as part of the Myanmar presidential amnesty to 6520 prisoners on Tuesday according to the President's OfficeThe two Yangonbased journalists Wa Lone and Kyaw Soe Oo from Reuters were sentenced to seven years in September 2018 for breaching the Official Secrets ActThey were arrested for possessing important and secret government documents with regard to the Rakhine state and security forces and intending to send them to the foreign news agencyMeanwhile the presidential amnesty has brought the total prisoners released since Myanmar's traditional New Year last month to 23019

For the upcoming 2019 Big Data Expo which will be held in Guiyang capital of Southwest China's Guizhou province from May 26 to 29 a promotional video has been playing on advertising billboards in Times Square New York from May 1Over four weeks starting from May 1 the 12 billboards in Times Square will play the 30second promotional video 18 times per day to showcase Guiyang's achievements in big data while also inviting the entire world to attend the Big Data ExpoThe promotional video is also appearing on China Central Television's CCTV channels 3 4 and 13 from May 1 to 29

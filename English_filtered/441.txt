Entries of the 2019 China Tourism Commodities Contest went on display at Yiwu International Expo Center in Zhejiang province These welldesigned commodities combining the characteristics of the destination and modern aesthetics are popular souvenirs among touristsIt's one of the various events of the 14th China Yiwu Cultural Products Trade Fair and the 11th China International Tourism Commodities Fair which were jointly held between April 27 and 30 in the venueWithin an area of 100000 square meters there are 1277 exhibitors from home and abroad showcasing various commodities in 4192 exhibition stalls There are 10 exhibition areas such as intangible culture heritage animation comics and games and creative cultural souvenirs

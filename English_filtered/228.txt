Divock Origi was the unlikely hero as Liverpool pulled off the greatest semifinal comeback in Champions League history with a 40 secondleg victory to complete a stunning 43 aggregate triumphThe odds were stacked heavily against Liverpool after both Mohamed Salah and Roberto Firmino were ruled out with injury following a 30 firstleg defeat last week in SpainBut Origi gave Liverpool hope with a seventhminute goal at Anfield before Georginio Wijnaldum scored two quick secondhalf goals to level the tie setting the stage for the Belgian to strike the decisive goal 11 minutes from timeLiverpool will face Ajax or Tottenham in the final in Madrid on June 1AFP

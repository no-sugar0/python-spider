Nine Spanish artists from Aragon an autonomous community in northeastern Spain showcased their art during Art Beijing an exhibition held April 30 to May 3Featuring works by Francisco Goya Pablo Serrano Víctor Mira and Antonio Saura the exhibition highlighted the romanticism and surrealism in Spanish contemporary artYoung artists including Señor Cifrián Antonio Fernández Alvira Natalia Escudero and OPN Studio also brought recent creations to the event

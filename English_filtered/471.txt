About 20 years ago local villagers started to grow Sichuan pepper trees seeds of which are a popular seasoning found in Sichuan cuisine But they had to sell the farm produce at very low prices to dealers who came by motorbikesWith the highway we can sell peppers to factories directly at much better prices said FuWang Ruixin Party chief of Liuwantou village which administers Mashuping said the annual per capita income of the village was 10300 yuan last year much higher than the national poverty line of around 3000 yuan

CAIRO  Egypt announced on Saturday the discovery of part of an Old Kingdom cemetery during excavations carried out at the southeastern side of Giza Plateau near the Great PyramidsThe discovery was made by an Egyptian Archaeological Mission Mostafa Waziri secretarygeneral of the Supreme Council of Antiquities said in a statementWaziri explained that the team uncovered several Old Kingdom tombs and burial shafts and the oldest one is a limestone family tomb from the fifth dynasty

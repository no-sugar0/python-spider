BEIJING  Changan Ford Automobile Co Ltd a joint venture between China's Changan Automobile and American automaker Ford on Monday started to recall 19070 vehicles due to a risk of fire in defective starter motorsThe recall involves 2019 Escort models manufactured between Sept 1 2018 and Dec 15 2018 the State Administration for Market Regulation said Monday in a statement on its websiteA short circuit could occur in starter motors of defective vehicles leading to fire risks and safety hazards said the administrationChangan Ford will replace the defective parts free of charge it said

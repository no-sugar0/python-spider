XI'AN  Yan'an a former revolutionary base of the Communist Party of China CPC is no longer labeled poor as its last two impoverished counties have shaken off poverty the Shaanxi provincial government announced TuesdayYan'an hosted the then headquarters of the CPC and the center of the Communist revolution from 1935 to 1948 The city is now home to more than 350 sites related to the Chinese revolutionChinese President Xi Jinping has stressed the need to end poverty in old revolutionary base areas and improve local people's standard of living Xi said a welloff society is incomplete if people in old revolutionary base areas cannot shake off poverty

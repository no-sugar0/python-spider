KAMPALA  The Ugandan government on Tuesday said over 35 million people in several parts of the east African country are on the verge of starvationA government statement issued here said the affected people are in the eastern northeastern northern parts of the country and the cattle corridorThe statement said that Cabinet on Monday approved the release of 40 billion shillings about 108 million for emergency procurement of relief foodThe affected parts have faced a prolonged dry spell leading to crop failure and also affected animals according to the government

BEIJING  The number of registered nurses in China exceeded 4 million at the end of 2018 rising to 3 nurses per 1000 people the National Health Commission announced WednesdayRegistered nurses make up nearly 50 percent of the country's healthcare professionals said Jiao Yahui an official with the commission at a press conference ahead of International Nurses Day which falls on May 12The nursing services have been improved as a growing number of medical staff have expertise Jiao said adding that almost 70 percent of nurses hold junior college degrees or above

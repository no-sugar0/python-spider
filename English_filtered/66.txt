Since its establishment in 2000 the annual Prix Marcel Duchamp has become an influential art prize to support young artists living and working in France Works of the prize's previous winners and nominations were shown in Beijing in 2017 and 2018The third annual exhibition dedicated to the Prix Marcel Duchamp will be open at Beijing's Red Brick Art Museum on April 27 and run through June 26 On show will be the works of 14 prize winners and nominations to provide a representation of the contemporary art scene in FranceThe exhibition titled Your Footsteps are the Road for There is No Road will also be part of a series of cultural activities to mark the 55th anniversary of SinoFrench diplomatic ties

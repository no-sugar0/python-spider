Within one minute visitors can reach the top observation decks at Wushan Mountain in Chongqing to have a good look at the river thanks to an elevator with a vertical height of 222 metersThe elevator located in Wushan county of Chongqing has become known as the World's No 1 Elevator as well as the Goddess Ladder because Wushan Mountain is famous for its legends about a goddessConstruction began in 2016 with a total investment of 160 million yuan 237 million and the facility opened to the public in November 2018 It contains two highspeed twoway elevators moving at 4 meters per seconds Each elevator can carry a load of 1600 kilograms or approximately 21 passengers

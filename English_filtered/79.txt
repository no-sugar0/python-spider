BEIJING  China will construct a national emergency management big data application platform according to China Meteorological News on WednesdayThe platform led by the Ministry of Emergency Management will use the technologies of big data cloud computing and the Internet of Things to conduct online monitoring of major risks early warning and forecasting and efficiently handling disasters and accidentsThe platform will strengthen the coordination and information exchange with authorities in diplomacy industry and information public security civil affairs meteorology natural resources ecology and environment transport water resources agriculture and rural affairs as well as radio and television

BEIJING  China's fiscal revenue rose 53 percent year on year to over 727 trillion yuan about 107 trillion US dollars in the first four months of the year data showed WednesdayThe central government collected about 347 trillion yuan in fiscal revenue during the period up 43 percent year on year while local governments saw fiscal revenue rise 61 percent to around 38 trillion yuan according to statistics from the Ministry of Finance MOFChina's fiscal spending expanded 152 percent year on year to 757 trillion yuan during the first four months the MOF data showed
